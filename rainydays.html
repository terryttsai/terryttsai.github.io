RAINY DAYS
A man told me in a dream,
That if I don't chase after my dreams now,
That if I don't start running before the sun sets,
I will regret it for the rest of my days.
I will forever be lost in this beautiful world.
Having no place to call home.
Wandering aimlessly, wondering when I'll run out of time or strength.
What do you see in me?

That makes you stay, when I tell you to go?
Do you see a future in us?

When every voice tells us no?
